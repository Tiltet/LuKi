About us

In 2016, Vyacheslav Starvitsky and Elena Goretskaya opened the LU project; KI for taking pictures of places where they have fun together. After that, it grew from a
hobby into a real business, which they are still doing.
A lot has changed since then: another 20 people have joined the team who help them work to this day, a huge amount of support and feedback. We talked about more than 200 places throughout Russia, about the people in them and about
nature, we helped many people financially.
We are very glad that you stay with us and support us. We love you!
